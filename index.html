DECIBEL BREAKERS



A company name derived from the love of sound, loud sound.

It was established in 2019 and grown by its CEO Lethukukhanya Nhlapo,

a Soweto Born young Lad with a passion for Cars and music. Decibel Breakers

offers a variety of services within the automotive industry, from simple car

audio installation all the way through to custom paint jobs and engine rebuilds.

This is one company not limited to anything automotive and surely lives up to

breaking boundaries and horizons like it does Decibels.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality service within the automotive industry and make the world of driving better one vehicle at a time .we aim to fulfill client requests and provide them with a result that is as close to or beyond their dreams and desires

Our Plan

Our plan is to become easily accessible, and reliable . Growth is inevitable that why we plan to expand and eventually cover all sectors of the automotive industry

Our Vision

Changing how a Owner looks at their vehicle. Giving them a unique perspective to what their vehicle is and what it can be. Our vision is to work and make sure a client does not leave the same way they came
